Cacio e Pepe

What you'll need:

  • Six (6) ounces of spaghetti
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter (section into three 1-ounce slices)
  • 3/4 cup finely grated parmesan
  • 1/3 cup finely grated pecorino romano
  • Salt flakes and cracked pepper

Instructions

  1. Have all your ingredients ready.
  2. In a pot, bring 3 quarts of water to a boil. Season the water with salt.
  3. Add pasta and cook. Stirr occasionally.
  4. Melt 2 Tbsp of butter in another pot over medium heat. Add pepper and cook for 1 minute until toasted.
  5. Drain your pasta but keep 3/4 cup of this water as a reserve.
  6. Add 1/2 cup of your reserve water to a skillet. Bring to simmer and add both your pasta and remaining butter.
  7. Bring heat to low and add parmesan, stirring until melted and all the pasta is covered in sauce.
  8. Plate pasta and serve.
