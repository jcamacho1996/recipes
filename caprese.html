Caprese Salad

What you'll need:

  • Three (3) tomatoes (ripe)
  • 1lb. of fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil (1 bunch)
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt flakes and coarse pepper

Instructions

  1. Have all your ingredients ready.
  2. Cut the mozzarella into 1/4-inch thick slices.
  3. Cut your tomatoes around the same size as your cheese slices.
  4. Layer on a dish alternating slices of mozarella and tomato. At the same time, add a basil leaf in between each.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and add salt and pepper.
