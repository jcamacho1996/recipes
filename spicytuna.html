Spicy Tuna

What you'll need:

  • 4oz of sashimi-grade tuna
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp srichacha sauce (you can add more if you prefer spicier)
  • 1/2 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 2 green onions/scallions
  • 1 tsp toasted white sesame seeds

Spicy Mayonnaise (optional)

  • 1 Tbsp Japanese mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp srichacha sauce

Instructions

  1. Have all your ingredients ready.
  2. Chop the green onions while keeping the white and green parts separated. The green part will be our garnish. The white part will be mixed in with the tuna.
  3. Mix the srichacha sauce and sesame oil into a bowl.
  4. Cut the tuna into thin cubes.
  5. Add the tuna and green onions (white part) into your bowl and mix.
  6. Once mixed, pour the tuna into a dish and garnish with the green part of the green onions and add the sesame seeds.
  7. Add spicy mayo as desired.
  8. Serve over rice or wrap it in lettuce!
